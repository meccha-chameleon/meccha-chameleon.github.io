What Is It?

A Hide-and-Seek Revolution

Developed by Japanese indie creator lemorion_1224, Meccha Chameleon (めっちゃカメレオン — “Super Chameleon”) flips the hide-and-seek genre on its head. No props. No presets. You paint your disguise.

🎨

Body Painting System

Use the in-game palette and eyedropper tool to sample colours from the environment and paint your character in real time. Every match creates unique camouflage.

🕵️

Asymmetric Multiplayer

Split into Hiders and Seekers each round. Hiders blend in — Seekers must spot the subtle imperfections before time runs out.

🗺️

Diverse Stages

Kitchens, party rooms, art galleries, checkered floors and more. Plus full Steam Workshop support for community-created custom maps.

🎮

Three Game Modes

Normal hide-and-seek, Increasing Oni (infection-style), and Double mode where everyone hides then hunts. Something for every group.

🌐

Streamer Ready

Public lobbies, viewer participation, and developer-blessed streaming. Just include the game name in your title and you're good to go.

💬

Proximity Voice Chat

Talk to nearby players with built-in proximity voice (added in v1.1). Coordinate as a Seeker — or stay silent as a Hider.

Gameplay

How to Play

Every round follows a simple loop. Master the three pillars — Painting, Position, and Pose — to survive.

1

Join a Lobby

Host picks the map, mode, and privacy settings. Anyone can join public lobbies — perfect for stream audiences.

2–14 PlayersPublic or Private
2

Preparation Phase

Hiders start as pure white characters. You have limited time to explore the stage, pick a hiding spot, sample colours with the eyedropper, paint your body, and strike your pose.

Paint PaletteEyedropper ToolPose Menu
3

The Hunt

Seekers are released and must find every Hider before the timer expires. No flashlights — just sharp eyes. Tag suspicious players to eliminate them.

Systematic SearchAnomaly Detection
4

Victory or Defeat

Seekers win by finding everyone. Hiders win if at least one survives. In Increasing Oni mode, caught Hiders join the Seeker team — the pressure ramps up every round.

Win by SurvivalWin by Elimination

Game Modes

Three Ways to Play

The host picks the mode before each match. All modes use the same paint-and-hide core — the twist is in what happens after the hunt begins.

🎯

Normal

Classic hide-and-seek with fixed teams. Hiders paint and hide — Seekers hunt. The purest experience and best starting point for new players.

Recommended for Beginners
🧟

Increasing Oni

Infection-style snowball mode. Caught Hiders become Seekers, so the hunting team grows every round. Chaos scales beautifully with 8+ players.

Best with Large Groups
⚡

Double

Everyone hides first, then everyone hunts. The fastest full clear wins. The most competitive mode — rewards players strong at both painting and spotting.

Most Competitive
